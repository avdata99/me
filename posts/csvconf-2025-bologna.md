Estuve en Bologna, Italia, para CSVConf 2025. Estas son las notas que me llevé del evento.

This year we'll use 🤌separated values. We'll call it ISV (Italian emoji separated values). Excel won't get it anyway. #CSVConf

![ISV — Italian Separated Values](/img/twitter/csvconf-2025-bologna/1-isv.jpg)

[@jqnatividad](https://x.com/jqnatividad) proposes to upload data first allowing the CKAN Datapusher to infer the metadata and apply suggestions. It looks promising.

![Datapusher inferring metadata](/img/twitter/csvconf-2025-bologna/2-datapusher.jpg)
